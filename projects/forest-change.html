Forest Change Detector — Cameroon

A GEE + Python pipeline that monitors deforestation across Cameroon from 2018 to 2024 using Sentinel‑2 NDVI time series.

How it works

Cloud‑masked Sentinel‑2 imagery is used to compute annual NDVI composites. A baseline (2018) is compared with later years to detect persistent forest loss. Results are quantified by administrative commune.

Outputs

  • EUDR‑ready CSV with deforested area per commune
  • GeoTIFF change maps for visualisation
