🎓 Final-Year Dissertation · University of Yaoundé I · 2024

Integrated Geospatial Approach to EUDR Certification & Biodiversity Impact

Awarded with Highest Honors. This research addresses a critical gap in sustainable agriculture: moving beyond isolated environmental checks to a holistic, verifiable, and accessible monitoring system.

Core Innovation

An open‑source QGIS & Google Earth Engine workflow that simultaneously evaluates:

  • Deforestation Risk via NDVI vegetation analysis (Sentinel‑2)
  • Biodiversity Impact via proximity to IUCN threatened mammal species
  • Climate Vulnerability using ERA5 temperature change & CHIRPS precipitation anomaly
  • EUDR Domain Compliance through vector intersection with permanent forest boundaries

Key Outcome

The methodology generated automated "Sustainability Passport" PDF reports for 30 cocoa farms in Mbametkim, Cameroon – providing plot‑level evidence required for certifications like Rainforest Alliance and regulations like EUDR.
