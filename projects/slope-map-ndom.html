Slope Map of Ndom, Cameroon

A slope classification map of the Ndom area derived from a Digital Elevation Model (DEM). The map highlights varying terrain steepness, essential for land‑use planning, agriculture, and infrastructure risk assessment.

Methodology

DEM data processed in QGIS using the native Slope tool, reclassified into standard slope classes (flat, gentle, moderate, steep). The final map was designed with a clean, publication‑ready layout.
