Population Density Map — Cameroon

A choropleth map showing population density across all regions of Cameroon, built entirely with QGIS and open demographic data.

Why this matters

Clear, well‑designed population maps are essential for humanitarian planning, resource allocation, and environmental impact assessments. This map was shared on LinkedIn and received strong engagement from the GIS community.
